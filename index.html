Types Of Exercise

Strength Training Exercises Like Weightlifting And Resistance Training Help Build Muscle Strength And Increase Metabolism, While Functional Training Exercises Like TRX And Plyometrics Improve Overall Physical Performance And Agility.

Cardio

weight loss

Cardio Weight Loss Exercises, Such As Running, Cycling, And Rowing, Are Effective Ways To Burn Calories And Promote Weight Loss. These Exercises Elevate The Heart Rate And Increase Oxygen Consumption, Leading To A Higher Calorie Burn.

Biceps Brachii Muscle

Biceps Curl

Biceps Curls Are A Popular Strength Training Exercise That Targets The Biceps Muscle In The Upper Arm. This Exercise Involves Lifting Weights With A Palm-up Grip And Curling The Weights Towards The Shoulders.

Hamstring Muscles.

Leg Curl

Leg Curls Are A Strength Training Exercise That Targets The Hamstrings, The Muscles At The Back Of The Thigh. This Exercise Involves Lying Face Down On A Machine And Curling The Lower Legs Towards The Buttocks Against Resistance.

Strength Exercise

Squats

Squats Are A Compound Strength Training Exercise That Target Multiple Muscles In The Lower Body, Including The Glutes, Quads, And Hamstrings. This Exercise Involves Bending The Knees And Lowering The Hips While Keeping The Back Straight And The Feet Flat On The Ground.
